To Birth of a Hero

What is birth of a hero?

A series created by Quavon Hollis that takes place in a non-cannon DC universe. The story takes place with our protagonist Quavon Hollis, a young African American 18-year-old kid born in Harlem, NY who was raised in the Bronx, NY raised by his aunt due to his mother's drug addiction.

As he graduates high school and being the first in the family to do so, he decides to repair his relationship with his mom by moving in with her for his college semester. Which was a big mistake as he becomes more stressed out about the situation which causes his grades to drop soon leading to him receiving an email from the director of the school a retired Wayne Enterprises Executive Lucious "Gladue" Fox saying if a legitimate appeal isn't submitted then he will be removed from campus and owe all government's expenses.
