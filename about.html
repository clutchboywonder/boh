Clutch Boywonder

Quavon Hollis is a 23 year old African-American born in Harlem, New York. He comes from a rough background being the only child having a mother extremely addicted to drugs and a father who left his life at an early age. With his mother being unfit to take care of him. His aunt took him in and raised him as he was her own. With doing the best she could he'd still experience emotional trauma growing up as a kid but often distracted himself with videogames, comics, anime and ofcourse Hip Hop.

Quavon's love for anime & comics grew when he went to college, he began to work on a comic based on and real events in his life. So he decided to tell his story in 3 ways, Music, Animation & Comics based on DC Comics non-canon story line of becoming the 1st American Robin.

Kevin Rosario

Kevin Rosario is a 22 year animation college graduate who also graduated alongside Quavon Hollis & Nikko Griffin. He met Quavon in his animation class as well as Quavon was also always impressed by his work. Kevin is a talented young-old soul who's inspiration comes from his spirit and chill vibe. He believes in all things equal and peace. Kevin saw the creator of Birth Of A Hero (Quavon Hollis) project. Bits and pieces that Quavon would show of his alter ego "Clutch Boywonder" and he became the 1st founding member of Birth Of A Hero

Nikko Griffin

Nikko Griffin is a 23 year old college animation major graduate who recently graduated alongside Quavon Hollis. He is animation major who specializes in 3D animation and environmental artwork. He intends to own his own business for game design as he is a fast, witty and tough as nails when it comes to his craft. Nikko met the creator of Birth Of A Hero (Quavon Hollis) in his art class as Quavon was always struggled in drawing but Nikko would always be there to support him. The rest became history as the project brought them closer.

Ricky Wu
